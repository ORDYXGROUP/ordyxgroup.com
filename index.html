Common Questions

What you're probably wondering.

How long does an engagement take?

A typical engagement runs 90 days for the installation phase, followed by a monthly enforcement layer. In the first two weeks, we map the gaps. Weeks three through ten, the framework is installed. After that, we stay as your operational partner — not as a consultant who disappears after the document is delivered.

Do you work on-site or remotely?

The initial diagnostic is conducted remotely. Installation is predominantly on-site — systems built from a distance rarely hold. The enforcement phase operates in hybrid: monthly reviews, real-time escalation paths, and direct access to our team when it matters.

What if results don't materialise?

They do — when the conditions are met. Our guarantee requires two things: leadership commitment and honest cooperation. If those are present, results are not a question of possibility. They are a question of timing. If we assess your business and determine we cannot deliver, we tell you before we begin. We do not take engagements we cannot win.
